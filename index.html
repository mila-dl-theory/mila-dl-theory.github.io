Introduction

Mila's Deep Learning Theory Group is a discussion group is which we keep up with the latest research, collaborate and brainstorm to push the boundaries of deep learning theory. We meet every Thursday at 11:00 AM. The guidelines and schedule are provided in this website while poolings and internal discussions are conducted in Mila's Slack Channel .

Guidelines

Each meeting is maneged by one or a few leaders. Leaders present a quick overview of the topic in ~20 minutes followed by ~30 minutes for disucssions and questions. Finally ~10 minutes is allocated to summarize the the meeting and choose next week's topic.
